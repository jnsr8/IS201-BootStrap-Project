Jennifer Rasmussen Provo, Utah

Jeni was born in Illinois, spent 5 years in Bothell, Wasthington and at age 7 relocated to Hanford, California where she had a quiet, small town upbringing in the Central Valley. At age 18 she moved to Provo, Utah to attend Brigham Young University until she married her husband in 2000 and relocated to Salt Lake City.

After 10 years they relocated once more to Provo, Utah where they continue to raise their five children.
