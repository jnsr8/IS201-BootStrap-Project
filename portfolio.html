Space Design elements encompass all textures and colors. Using wood beams or slats on your ceiling draws the eye upwards and brings warmth to a space.

"The essence of interior design will always be about people and how they live. It is about the realities of what makes for an attractive, civilized, meaningful environment, not about fashion or what's in or what's out. This is not an easy job." -Roebert Hadley

Function Every space has a function. Living room, kitchen, bedroom, dining room, children's room. Design with its use in mind.

"A room should never allow the eye to settle in one place. It should smile at you and create fantasy." - Juan Montoya.

Color Don't hesitate to use bold colors in your design. Start with a simple palette and change out the accessories.

"The details are not the details. They make the design." - Charles Eames
